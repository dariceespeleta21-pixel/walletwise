10 Tips to Control Online Shopping

Practical strategies to break the cycle of compulsive buying

1

24-Hour Rule

Wait 24 hours before buying anything in your cart. Most impulse buys lose their appeal.

2

Unsubscribe & Unfollow

Remove shopping apps from your phone and unsubscribe from promotional emails.

3

Set Monthly Limits

Allocate a specific budget for "wants" vs "needs" and stick to it religiously.

4

Use Cash-Back Cards

Only shop using prepaid cards or cards with strict limits to prevent overspending.

5

Track Every Purchase

Log every online purchase immediately. Awareness is the first step to control.

6

Identify Triggers

Note when and why you shop. Is it boredom, stress, or social media influence?

7

Implement Cooling-Off Periods

For expensive items, implement a 7-day cooling-off period before purchasing.

8

Find Alternatives

Replace shopping with healthier habits like exercising, reading, or hobbies.

9

Accountability Partner

Share your spending goals with a friend who can help keep you accountable.

10

Celebrate Small Wins

Reward yourself for sticking to your budget, but not with more shopping!

Warning Signs of Shopping Addiction

Hiding purchases from family

Shopping to relieve stress

Feeling guilt after shopping

Maxing out credit cards

Shopping instead of paying bills

Lying about spending

If you recognize these signs, it's time to take control. WalletWise can help.
